Press A Key to Start

Instructions

  • Press any key on your keyboard to begin the game.
  • One of the four buttons will glow briefly. Remember the sequence.
  • Click the button that glowed first.
  • After that, a new button will glow, so click the buttons in the order they glowed.
  • The sequence will get longer each round.
  • You must remember and repeat the entire sequence correctly.
  • If you click the wrong button, the game ends.
  • Try to beat your previous score!
  • Enjoy testing your memory and see how long you can keep up!
